  • User Functions

    This section focuses on the options specific to the user functions of meeting rooms.

    • Chatting in a meeting

      The chat feature is enabled by default and allows users to communicate with all members of the meeting using the chat function.

    • Raising my hand

      The Raise hand feature is enabled by default and allows users to raise and lower hands throughout the meeting to request to speak, answer a poll, or as a form of silent acknowledgment.

    • Muting and unmuting

      The mute and/or unmute feature allows users to mute their microphone to prevent people from hearing you and then unmute when you want to speak.

    • Enabling and disabling video

      The enable and/or disable video feature allows users to enable or disable the video settings.

    • Presenting

      The start presenting feature allows users to share what they are working on or presenting with attendees.

    • Leaving a meeting

      The leave feature allows users to hang up and leave the meeting.

    • Choosing how participants are displayed

      The participants feature allows users to view participants and perform group functions.
