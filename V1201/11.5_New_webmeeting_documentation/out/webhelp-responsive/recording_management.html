      This section focuses on the additional controls that are available to meeting owners and moderators.

      • Admitting participants during a meeting

        The waiting room feature requires participants to remain in a waiting area before they are admitted in to the meeting. When enabled, moderators can control when a participant joins a meeting. Moderators can allow authenticated users to bypass the waiting room and go directly in to the meeting. For more information on how to enable the waiting room, refer to t_create_moderated.html and meetings_moderated_management.html.

      • Start a YouTube live stream session

        Meeting moderators can start a live stream on a YouTube channel. Live stream sessions are typically lecture-style and do not allow participant interaction.

      • Record a meeting

        You can use the Start recording feature to record the meeting.

Recordings management - FAQ's

When the user records a meeting, they can access the link from the meeting room in the meeting client, group chat and one-on-one chat in the webchat client, and meeting recording link from the email.

This topic has some frequently asked questions about the all-new meetings recordings management.

Where will the meeting recording links be available?

The meeting recording links will show only in the owner's recording list irrespective of whether he shared it or not.

How can the meeting owner share a meeting recording?

When the meeting owner stops the recording, a confirmation dialog box displays that will ask the owner to stop the recording or not. By default, the option to share the recording in the group chat is checked. The meeting owner can uncheck it based on their preference. This setting will remain the same for all the future recordings for that meeting room until the owner changes it in the next meeting.

Will the meeting moderator get the option to share the recording?

When a moderator stops recording a confirmation dialog displays with the option to share in disable state. The default meeting recording setting set by the owner for the meeting room will reflect here.

If the meeting owner sets the option to share the recording, a single meeting recording link appears in the group chat.

Can a guest user access the meeting link?

No, if a guest user clicks on the meeting recording link, they cannot access it.

Where can the authenticated users access the recording link from?

The authenticated users can access the recording link from the meeting room in the meeting client, group chat and one-on-one chat in the webchat client, and meeting recording link from the email.

What if the owner who starts the meeting leaves or disconnects from the meeting?

The moderator on behalf of the owner can stop the recording. However, the moderator cannot change the default sharing option that is set by the owner.

Does the recording stop when everyone leaves the meeting?

Recording stops automatically when the last person leaves the meeting. Based on the default sharing option set by the owner, the recording link will either be shared or not shared with all authenticated meeting attendees.

What if someone forgets to leave the meeting?

The recording automatically stops when there is a silence of 10 mins. Based on the default sharing option set by the owner, the recording link will either be shared or not shared with all authenticated meeting attendees.
