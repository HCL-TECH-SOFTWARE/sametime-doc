  • Basics

    After you log in, you can exchange text chats with your chat partners and more.

    • System requirements

      This document provides minimum system requirements for HCL Sametime 11.6. Higher maintenance levels, such as fix packs and service packs, may be supported as they become available.

    • Starting Sametime

      After you click the link below, you can start the HCL Sametime® Web Chat client application.

    • Starting a chat

      When your contact list is populated with the names of individual contacts and groups, it is easy to start a chat with them.

    • Display availability status

      Display your location and contact information in your status card when you are online. Others see this information when they hover their cursor over your name in the contact list or engage in an online chat with you.

    • Adding contacts to my contact list

      The contact list is a list of people with whom you frequently chat.

    • Starting a Meeting?

      You can start a meeting through the Meetings option from the drop-down menu, or click the Launch invite and send button to instantly send a meeting invite in the conversation box.

    • Keyboard shortcuts and other accessibility features

      Accessibility features help users who have a disability, such as restricted mobility or limited vision, to use information technology products successfully. HCL® strives to provide products with usable access for everyone regardless of age or ability.
