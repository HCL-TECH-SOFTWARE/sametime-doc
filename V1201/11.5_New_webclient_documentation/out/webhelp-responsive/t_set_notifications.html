    • How do I

      After you log in, you can exchange text chats with your chat partners and more.

      • Setting your status

        Your online status includes your availability and status message. The top of the application menu displays this information . Tap your current status to make changes. Other logged-in Sametime users can see your online status.

      • Setting notifications

        You can define how you want to receive notifications for chats, announcements, and calls.

      • Configuring a mobile device for chats

        You can use your mobile device as your chat client using the QR code provided in the Settings menu.

      • Organizing contacts

        Add people to your contact list, give them nicknames, and organize them into groups.

      • Filtering chats

        Several predefined filters are available to sort chats.

      • Starting a chat

        You can start a chat with anyone within your chat community.

      • Starting a group chat

        You can start a chat with a group that is part of your contacts.

      • Inviting others to an existing chat

        If you are in a text chat with one chat partner, you can quickly invite someone from your contact list to join the chat. A chat with more than two chat partners is referred to as a group chat.

      • Sending an announcement

        You can send a message to all the online members of a group simultaneously.

      • Calling a contact

        You can call a contact or group from the chat window.

    • Meetings

      If you are using HCL Sametime Premium, you can create, start, and participate in Sametime meetings.

Setting notifications

You can define how you want to receive notifications for chats, announcements, and calls.

About this task

You can control the type of notification that you receive for each of the following.
  • 1-to-1 chat messages
  • Group chat messages
  • New group chats
  • Announcements
  • Calls

Procedure

  1. Click the menu icon ( ) in the navigator and select Settings > Notifications.
  2. Click the down arrow next to the event that you want to set a notification and select the notification type.
    Off
    Receive no notifications.
    Banner
    Receive a banner displayed at the top of the chat window.
    Sound
    Receive an audio sound only.
    Banner and Sound
    Receive both an audio sound and banner.
  3. When finished, click the return arrow () to return to Settings.
  4. Click the return arrow () to return to the home page.
