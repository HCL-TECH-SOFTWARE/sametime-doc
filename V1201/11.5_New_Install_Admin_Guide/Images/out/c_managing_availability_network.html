Managing availability on the network

You can log in, change your status, and set up alerts that notify you when others become available.

Logging in

After you start IBM Connections Chat, the login panel is displayed.

Using the main screen

The main screen gives you access to all of IBM Connections Chat functionality.

Adding contacts

A contact is someone whom you have added to one or more of your groups. When you are online, by default only contacts that are also online are visible.

Creating groups

You can organize your contacts by placing them into groups. You can create a group when adding a contact.

Setting your online status

Your online status indicates your availability. Your availability is indicated to you by an icon in the left Action Bar on tablets, or in one of the top tabs on a phone. People who are logged in can see your online status.

Setting up notifications

You can set notifications to alert you with sound, speech, vibration, or light when someone begins a Connections chat.
