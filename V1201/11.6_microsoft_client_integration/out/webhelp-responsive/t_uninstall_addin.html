Removing the Sametime Meetings Add-in

The uninstall the Sametime meetings add-in, users must remove the custom add-in the users added to Microsoft outlook.

Procedure

  1. Navigate to your My add-in window. Click the Get add-ins icon located in the task bar.
  2. On the Add-ins for Outlook window, click My Add-ins.
    The Sametime Meetings add-in is located in Custom Addins section.
  3. Click the Manage Add-in icon (within the Sametime Meeting tile and select Remove.
